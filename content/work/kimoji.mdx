When Kim Kardashian wanted to give her fans a new way to communicate, we built Kimoji — a custom emoji keyboard app that became one of the most talked-about app launches in history.

The launch reportedly crashed the Apple App Store and the app reached the top of the charts within hours, reportedly generating one million dollars per minute during its peak.

---

### The Product

Kimoji gave fans hundreds of Kim-inspired emojis, stickers, and GIFs built for the way her audience actually communicates online.
