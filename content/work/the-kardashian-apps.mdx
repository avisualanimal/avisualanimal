Before influencer culture dominated the internet, the Kardashian-Jenner family launched five personalized lifestyle apps that gave fans an unprecedented level of access. We built the platform from concept to launch.

---

### The Concept

Each sister had her own app, her own voice, and her own community. We designed a unified UX framework with distinct editorial identities for each of the five apps.

![Platform architecture diagram](/images/work/the-kardashian-apps/diagram.png)

---

### Apple Partnership

The apps launched exclusively at the Apple SoHo store — a moment that brought all five sisters together for the first time in a retail environment.

---

### Performance

At launch, the apps dominated the App Store charts.
