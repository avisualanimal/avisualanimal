When Microsoft entered the tablet market, they needed a launch that would announce their arrival with the same force as their ambition. We developed a layered guerrilla advertising campaign that teased the tablet's arrival before anyone knew what it was.

---

### Visual Strategy

The campaign began with geometry. Abstract shapes appeared across cities — on transit, on streets, on walls — without any brand attribution.

![Street-level advertisement](/images/work/microsoft-surface/street-1.jpg)

![Street-level advertisement](/images/work/microsoft-surface/street-2.jpg)

---

### A Layered Reveal

The rollout was engineered as a sequence. Geometric shapes were introduced progressively across cities like New York and Paris, building in complexity and presence until the final reveal of the Microsoft Surface.

![Layered reveal diagram](/images/work/microsoft-surface/reveal-layers.png)

---

### An Overnight Success

The campaign sparked massive buzz before the product was ever officially shown. It dominated streets and transit areas in major cities worldwide.
