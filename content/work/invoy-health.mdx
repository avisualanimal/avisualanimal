Invoy is a metabolic tracking system that uses simple breath tests to give people an unprecedented window into how their body burns fat. We partnered with Invoy from the earliest stages to define the brand and build the product experience from the ground up.

---

### Brand Values

The challenge was finding the visual language that could hold both worlds at once — the precision of clinical science and the warmth of personal guidance.

![Brand values visualization](/images/work/invoy-health/brand-values.png)

---

### Product Design

The app needed to guide users through a process that felt foreign — breath testing — while making the results feel immediately meaningful.

---

### Member Journey

Progress in metabolic health is not linear. The member journey we designed acknowledges this — surfacing wins and patterns that keep people engaged even when the scale doesn't move.

![Member journey graph](/images/work/invoy-health/member-journey.png)

---

### Coaching Engine

The coaching portal was built to give health coaches a real-time view of every member's progress.

![Coaching engine diagram](/images/work/invoy-health/coaching-engine.png)

---

### Clinical & Physician Tools

Invoy's clinical network required a complete suite of physician-facing tools — from kiosk registration flows to a science communication system.

![Physician tablet interface](/images/work/invoy-health/doctor-tablet.png)
